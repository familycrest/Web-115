This page allows you to make your own meal plan. You can print your meal plan or you can generate as many meal plans as you want on a popup web page. Simply put in new information and then press "Generate Meal Plan" to create a new meal plan as many times as you want. You don't have to change the information to make a new meal plan that way, either. Simply put fill in as many of the fields as you want. To generate a meal plan on the popup or to print one you have to enter an email, but the rest of the information is optional.
